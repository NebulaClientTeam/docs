---
title: GUI Builder & Manager
description: Manage routes visually through the Nebula GUI.
---

Open the Nebula GUI and navigate to the **Routes** screen to manage routes visually. The GUI provides:

- **Route list** with search and filtering by type (`simple`, `warp`, `island`, `mining`, `multi`).
- **Route details panel** showing waypoint count, type, and configuration.
- **Create/Edit** routes with a visual waypoint builder.
- **Test/Play** buttons to execute a route directly.
- **Render toggle** to preview routes in-world.
- **Import/Export** buttons for sharing routes.
- **Duplicate and Delete** options.
- **Anchor block selection mode** for mining routes — lets you click blocks in-world to mark them as anchors.

For Multi Routes, the GUI shows all sub-routes within the group and lets you inspect each one individually.
